Dear parent

DinoDent is a cash practice with credit card facilities and we do not charge medical aid tariffs. Therefore, you first need to settle the account immediately after the appointment and then claim from your medical aid.

It is important to send the quotation to your medical aid to confirm how much they will pay and what portion they will not cover. Please remember that you are liable for the full amount.

A 50% deposit needs to be settled before your child receives treatment under general anesthesia of sedation. The rest of the account is due 30 days after treatment. These patients need to come for two weeks (free) and three month follow-up appointments.

If your child receives three or more fillings then he/she is considered high risk to develop dental caries and needs to come for four consecutive three month follow-up appointments. After the one year period, if no fillings had to be replaced or new cavities have developed, then we can assess him/her to come six monthly instead of three monthly.

All other patients should come visits every six months.

If a filling falls out, please contact us to replace it. I provide a six month guarantee on restorations done. However, you lose this guarantee if you miss your prescribed dental visits. I will not be liable for future dental treatment after this period has passed, however I will gladly provide the necessary treatment (at normal rates).
